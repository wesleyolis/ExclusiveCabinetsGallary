Welcome to Exclusive Cabinets Online!
 
Exclusive cabinets & Components was established in July 1989, manufacturing and installing kitchens and built-in units for the domestic market. This business was expanded into office furniture with such success that we now specialize in office and domestic furniture.
 
We operate out of a company-owned 1400m2 factory in Blackheath and our experience and knowledge of working in particle board and laminates has made us a leading supplier of office and domestic furniture and components to the retail, corporate and public sector markets.
 
We pride ourselves on being able to deliver - on time - furniture that our clients know will meet their requirements in terms of design, colour, quality and continuity of supply.
 
We at Exclusive Cabinets & Components would like to express our thanks and gratitude to all our clients and suppliers who have loyally supported us over the past 15 years and look forward to continuing this close association.
